Healthy food

Healthy food is defined as a variety of foods that provide the body with the nutrients necessary to maintain its health 

            Healthy food is defined as a variety of foods that provide the body with the nutrients
            necessary to maintain its health, provide it with energy, and generate a good feeling for it.
            These elements include carbohydrates , proteins, and fats, in addition to minerals, vitamins,
            and water. A healthy diet helps prevent malnutrition in all its forms, and helps prevent
            noncommunicable diseases, such as heart disease, diabetes , and cancer. In fact, healthy eating
            practices begin at an early stage of a person's life; Breastfeeding helps with healthy growth 
            and cognitive development, as well as its other long-term health benefits, such as reducing the risk
             of obesity.Or being overweight and some noncommunicable disease later in life.
